Fixed my issue with passwords not being saved on the Mac.

1. Shutdown Chrome
2. Go to ~username/Library
3. Delete Google folder from ~/Library/Application Support.
4. Delete the following folders from ~/Library/Caches:
com.google.Chrome.helper.EH
com.google.GoogleDrive
com.google.gpautobackup
com.google.Keystone.Agent
com.google.SoftwareUpdate
Google
5. Delete ~/Library/Google
6. Startup Chrome and setup sync again
