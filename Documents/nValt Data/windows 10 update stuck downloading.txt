1. net stop wuauserv
2. net stop bits
3. re-run 'net stop wuauserv' until it's completely stopped (doesn't always stop)
4. delete everything from C:\Windows\SoftwareDistribution
5. keep deleting and making sure 'net stop wuauserv' is stopped
6. after all is deleted
7. net start wuauserv
8. net start bits
9. run windows update again
