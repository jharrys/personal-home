### How to unload spotlight on mac os x; some report it can conflict with Time Machine (old report)

    * to stop

    sudo launchctl unload -w /System/Library/LaunchDaemons/com.apple.metadata.mds.plist
    
    * to start

    sudo launchctl load -w /System/Library/LaunchDaemons/com.apple.metadata.mds.plist
    
    
reported by: http://www.captaindebug.com/2012/10/apple-mac-time-machine-runs-very-slowly.html
