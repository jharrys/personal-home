## Removing a project with all associated components
#### (jira 7.3.6)

**This requires admin rights**

1. Delete project (e.g., KEY=HCAR)
2. Click on **GEAR -> System**
3. Select **Issues** in horizontal top menu
4. Choose **Workflow schemes**
    1. look for HCAR and **delete** as many as there are
6. Choose **Workflow**
    1. look for HCAR and **delete** as many as there are 
8. Choose **Issue type screen schemes**
    1. look for HCAR and **delete** as many as there are
10. Choose **Screen schemes**
    1. look for HCAR and **delete** as many as there are
12. Choose **Screens**
    1. look for HCAR and **delete** as many as there are
14. Choose **Issue type schemes**
    1. look for HCAR and **delete**
